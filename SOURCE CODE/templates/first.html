Common Types of Skin Cancer

Basal Cell Carcinoma

The most common type, often looks like a flesh-colored bump or a pinkish patch of skin.

Squamous Cell Carcinoma

Often appears as a firm, red nodule, a flat lesion with a scaly crust, or a sore that doesn't heal.

Melanoma

The most serious type. Often develops in a mole or appears as a new, unusual-looking growth.
